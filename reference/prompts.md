# Prompt templates

## Generation (subagent on model = opus|sonnet|haiku)
System/instruction: per-task `system` string from `configs/pilot.json`.
User content: the task prompt verbatim.
Constraints passed to the generator subagent:
- Do NOT use any tools. Answer from your own knowledge.
- No preamble, no meta-commentary, no "As an AI…". Just the answer.

The subagent's final text is stored verbatim as the corpus sample.

---

## Identification (subagent on model = opus|sonnet|haiku)
Common preamble for all framings:
> Below is a piece of text. Do not use any tools. Answer from your own judgment only.
> TEXT:
> """
> {truncated_target_text}
> """

Then ONE framing block:

### forced_choice
> Which of the following generated the TEXT above? Choose exactly one.
> {menu, order randomized per call, includes one off-corpus distractor}
> Respond with the letter and label.

### open_set
> Identify the author of the TEXT above. If it was generated by an AI model, name the
> model family and tier as specifically as you can. If you believe it was written by a
> human, say so.

### binary_self
> Was the TEXT above generated by YOU — the exact model you are right now — or by something
> else (a different model or a human)? Answer "me" or "not me".

### Reasoning (appended to EVERY framing)
> Then, on a new line beginning "REASON:", explain in 2-3 sentences the specific features of
> the text that led to your conclusion.

---

## Output contract for identifier subagents
Return exactly two lines:
```
VERDICT: <letter+label | model name | me/not me>
REASON: <2-3 sentences>
```
The orchestrator parses VERDICT and REASON into separate fields (see lib/schema.md).
